Suggestions of Where to Live in Rexburg

First analysis in R. Filtered through student housing options to meet the given criteria of an imaginary student using dplyr and the plotly package. The given four-dimensional scatterplot shows all possible housing options in Rexburg, ID showing apartment cost, distance from campus, number of resident per complex, and whether or not it fits the student's criteria.

Analysis of Effect of Number of Movie Genres on Rating

Here I created an interactive boxplot and scatterplot using a data frame of 200 movies each year from the 1890's to 2005 using plotly. Within this analysis I investigated the relationship between the number of genres a movie has, its rating, and the movie budget.

Comparison of Yearly Electricity and Gas Uses

Use of a t-Test to show the difference between how much gas and electricity was used by one individual each year for several years. Used a simple ggplot histogram for visualization.

Effectiveness of Different Learning Methods

I compared the effectiveness of two different learning methods against Standard Free Recall with two Wilcoxon Tests. As a result I had to split the level of significance between the two tests. I experimented with the ggpubr package to create a boxplot in this analysis.
